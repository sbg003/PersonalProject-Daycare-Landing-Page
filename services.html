Services

Meals are provided and included in cost (unless you would like to provide for special needs). Includes breakfast, lunch, snacks, and water (dependent on hours)

Naptime/quiet-time given daily at consistent time, with an age-appropriate safe sleep space provided for each child (pack-n-play, mat, etc)

Outside play in fenced backyard or quiet, cul-de-sac front yard given each day for at least 20min

Preschool activities are taught each day based on age of child

Potty training can be provided if child is ready and needs it while in my care.

Transportation can be provided if it doesn't interfere with my older child's bus times. Will be an additional cost

Pricing

I strive to keep my prices affordable and am willing to negotiate.

Price will depend on number of children and hours/days per week needed. Contact me to discuss your needs!

Prices typically begin at $220* per week for first child, and $100 per additional child. *Dependent on your hours, current market rate, tax reporting, etc.
